About

I’m Michael Cevallos, a software and web engineer who loves turning messy problems into clean, reliable products. I started my career in construction, where I learned to think on my feet and deliver under pressure. I later served in the Marine Corps as an infantryman specializing in rockets and explosives—an experience that sharpened my discipline, teamwork, and bias for action. In 2022 I completed CodeUp’s Full-Stack Development bootcamp and have been building continuously ever since, shipping 49+ GitHub projects and contributions. I’ve strengthened my foundation with ACI Learning certifications in CompTIA Security+ and ITIL 4 Foundation, and I’m currently pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science at Southern New Hampshire University.

My toolkit includes Java, JavaScript/TypeScript, React, HTML, CSS, SQL, Spring Boot, Python, MySQL, and Git/GitHub. I’m comfortable across the stack—designing REST APIs, modeling data, building responsive UIs, and deploying production-ready code. I thrive in Agile teams, write maintainable, well-tested code, and care deeply about usability and performance. I’m looking for a role where I can keep learning, contribute to a strong engineering culture, and ship software that actually helps people.
